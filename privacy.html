Privacy Policy

This application is a private, single-user tool operated solely by its owner, Parsa Banaei, for personal use.

What it accesses: With the owner's explicit Google authorization, the application reads the owner's own Gmail messages from specific newsletter senders, in order to extract topics for the owner's own social media posts.

How data is used: Email content is used only to generate draft posts for the owner's review. Data is processed privately on the owner's own infrastructure.

What is shared: Nothing. No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

What is stored: Only the minimal content needed to produce the owner's drafts, kept on the owner's own private systems.
